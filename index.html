About the Bermuda Darts League

The Darts League brings together all levels of players from across the Island for a fast-paced, competitive season of high-quality darts. Our goal is to provide a professional, welcoming environment for all players and fans of the game.

Each week, teams face off in home and away fixtures, battling for points that decide the league table and playoff positions. Matches follow official rules, with formats including 501 and Competitions to test accuracy, consistency, and composure under pressure.

League Rules

📄 League Rules PDF

These rules outline how matches in the Bermuda Darts League are played and how points are awarded. Captains and players should review them regularly.

  • Match Format – Standard league nights consist of 1001, 501 singles, 501 doubles, as agreed by the fixtures.
  • Scoring – 501 legs are straight start, double finish unless otherwise stated by the competition rules.
  • Arrivals – Teams should arrive at least 15 minutes before the scheduled start time to allow for warm‑up and registration.
  • Conduct – Respectful behaviour is required at all times. Unsporting conduct may result in warnings, leg penalties, or match forfeiture.
  • Results – Captains are responsible for confirming and submitting results immediately after each match night.

For any disputes or clarifications, please contact the league committee. Updates to these rules will be communicated to all captains before they take effect.
